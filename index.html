Monkeys

All about Monkeys:

Monkeys are a group of primate animals other than human beings that are furry and live in warm regions.
Monkeys are known as Simians, and all animals in the group now known as simians are counted as monkeys except the apes.

Here are some facts about Monkeys:
    • Just like young children, monkeys have a high IQ.
    • Illnesses can be spread to people by monkeys. Such as Monkeypox.
    • Monkeys and apes are not the same but are related.
    • To interact with one another, monkeys engage in grooming rituals.

Special things about Monkeys:

  • Monkeys use facial expressions, body movements, and various noises to communicate with each other.
  • Something that Monkeys love the most are bananas.
